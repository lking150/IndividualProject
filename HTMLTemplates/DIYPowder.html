DIY Powders

How to Create Your Own Colored Powder

Things you will need:

~ Oil Pastels in any color(s) you want (See Image 1)

~ Clear or White Dip Powder

~ Nail Dremel

~ Container to mix/store the powder (See Image 2)

What to do:

~ Using the nail dremel, grind off a little bit of the oil pastels into the container. (See Image 3)

~ Add in a couple spoon fulls of clear dip powder to the container.

~ Using the nail dremel again, carefully mix the pastel and powder together. (See Image 4)

~ You may need to seal the container and give it a good shake to finalize the mixture.

~ Make a swatch stick of the color to know just what the powder looks like (See Image 5).
