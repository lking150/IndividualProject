Step-By-Step Directions

Nail Prep

Step 1. Put a small dab of cuticle remover onto each nail and gently massage it into the cuticle

Step 2. After waiting 3 minutes, use a cuticle pusher to gently push back any cuticle on your nail

Step 3. If needed, using cuticle scissors, carefully cut back any excess cuticle

Step 4. Wash your hands using any degreasing soap (Dawn dish soap is the most commonly used)

Step 5. Taking a cotton ball soaked in 100% acetone, wipe off each nail

Applying the Dip Powder

Step 1. Apply a thin layer of the Pro Base to the nail

Step 2. Dip the finger into the powder (See helpful links section for different methods) and brush off any excess powder on the nail

Step 3. Repeat Steps 1 through 3 AT LEAST two times

Step 4. Apply a generous amount of the Activator to each nail and let sit for 2 minutes

Step 5. Shape and buff the nails to achieve a smooth nail

Step 6. Rinse your hands with JUST water

Step 7. Apply another coat of Activator and let sit for 1 minute

Step 8. Apply a quick 2-3 stroke layer of the Finish Gel to each nail and let sit for 1 minute

Step 9. Apply a more thorough layer of the Finish Gel, ensuring that you cap all your edges and let sit for 2 minutes

Removing the Dip Powder via "Baggy" method

Step 1: File off the shine of all of your nails

Step 2: Using a plastic baggy, pour enough 100% acetone to soak your nails and a dryer sheet or paper towel

Step 3: Get a bowl of as hot of water as you can stand and place the baggy on top of the water

Step 4: Soak your nails in the acetone and after 2 minutes begin using the dryer sheet to scrub the powder off your nails

Step 5: Continue to soak and scrub your nails until all of the dip powder has been removed

Step 6: Wash your hands thoroughly and moisturize with lotion and cuticle oil
