DIY Powders

How to Create Your Own Colored Powder

Things you will need:

~ Oil Pastels (in any color(s) you want)

~ Clear Dip Powder

~ Nail Dremel

~ Container to mix/store the powder

What to do:

~ Using the nail dremel, grind off a little bit of the oil pastels into the container.

~ Add in a couple spoon fulls of clear dip powder to the container.

~ Using the nail dremel again, carefully mix the pastel and powder together.

~ You may need to seal the container and give it a good shake to finalize the mixture.

~ Come up with a fun and clever name to go with your new color(s).
